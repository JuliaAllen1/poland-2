    This website is for an AP Human Geography project about Poland, the nation in Central Europe.

    Poland is in the perceptual region of "Central Europe", along with the functional region of the European Union.
    The Capital City is Warsaw, located at 52.2297° N, 21.0122° E. From South Carolina, Warsaw is ~4,841.20 miles away,
    significantly to the northeast. The climate is temperate, with warm/hot summers, cold winters, and otherwise is in between.
    Poland cover just under 121,000 square miles total area, with the highest point being the peak of Mt. Rysy (8,199 ft.), and
    the lowest being Raczki Elbląskie(-7 ft).
